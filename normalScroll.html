Professional Life

The Healer

Edgar Karori is a licensed physiotherapist with a passion for sports rehabilitation and functional recovery. Trained at a top institution and registered with the Kenya Medical Board, he combines clinical precision with a patient-first approach to ensure every individual gets back to doing what they love.

Beyond the Clinic

The Person

Off the clock, Edgar is a proud rugby player, a Gen-Z with the energy of a whole team, and a deeply patriotic Kenyan. He believes that understanding life beyond the clinic makes him a better therapist — because healing is as much about the mind and spirit as the body.
